Shih-Tzu

Tzu meaning lion or little lion is a toy breed beloved for their lap dog tendencies and fun loving personalities.
The playful pups are excellent family dogs, but pet parents must be devoted to socialization and a regular grooming schedule to maintain their long coat.
Shih Tzus generally have a lifespan of 10 to 18 years, but they are prone to a handful of health conditions.
The cost of managing or treating these conditions should be taken into consideration before purchasing or adopting this breed.
